One-on-one sessions

One-on-one sessions with instructors are mentoring sessions that can be scheduled via an online system. Approximately a combined total of 30h are available every week for bookings like this. This totals around 90 minutes per student per week, meaning these meetings should be very concise and specific. Mostly they should be around clarifying concepts or resolving issues that were not possible to solve in the classroom community and Sharing & Consolidation meetings.
