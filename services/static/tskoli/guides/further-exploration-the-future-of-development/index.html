Guide: Further Exploration - The future of Development

Description

The student should do research to try and find out what are some topics that will be commonplace in a few years in the world of web development but that are still not. One or a few topics must be chosen and a demo must be created to showcase them.

Knowledge

  • Understand the evolving nature of web development

Skills

  • Be able to draw a picture of what the future could look like in web development

Topics

Deliverable: Report and Demo

Group arrangement

This work must be done individually.

 

Minimum requirements

  • A research report must be created and published
  • A demo must be created to showcase the research topic(s)

 

Deliverables

  • Link to research results article
  • Link to demo application
  • Sharing session
